** start of index.html ** Protein Peanut Butter Ice Cream

My Ultra Secret Recipe Page

A delicious and easy recipe for peanut butter ice cream with fewer calories and more protein. It won’t taste like whey, and it will satisfy your cravings with just one-third of the pot.

Ingredients

  • Canderel Original Low Calorie Sweetener (8g)
  • Alpro Almond No Sugars Plant-Based Chilled Drink, Vegan & Dairy Free (360ml)
  • Alpro Soya High Protein Plant-Based Long Life Drink, Vegan & Dairy Free (360ml)
  • Angel Delight Chocolate Flavour (12g)
  • Reese’s Giant Peanut Butter Bar, Milk Chocolate Coated Peanut Butter (3 chunks)
  • PhD Nutrition Diet Whey Lean Protein Powder | Vanilla Crème (45g)

You’ll also need the following equipment:

  • Ninja CREAMi Deluxe (for these portions; adjust to 0.75× on smaller model)
  • Handheld blender, standard blender, or electric stirrer
  • A freezer
  • A little patience

Instructions

  1. Pour the almond and soy plant milks into the CREAMi container.
  2. Add the whey protein powder and sweetener.
  3. Stir in the Angel Delight chocolate flavour until smooth.
  4. Freeze the mixture overnight.
  5. After freezing, run room-temperature water along the outside of the container for a few seconds.
  6. Process in the Ninja CREAMi using the “Ice Cream” or “Lite Ice Cream” setting.
  7. Add the Reese’s chunks and re-run the “Mix-in” cycle.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
